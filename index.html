Ying Chen（陈颖）

Ph.D student in Computional Mathematics
Center for Simulation of Complex Systems
School of Mathematics and Statistics
Huazhong University of Science and Technology
     


Brief Biography: Ying Chen is a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), she obtained the B.S degree in Information and Computational Science from Henan Normal University (HNU).

Research Interests: Lattice Boltzmann Method; Finite Difference Method; Computational Fluid Dynamics​; Partial Differential Equations (Convection-Diffusion Equations, Hyperbolic Conservation Laws).
